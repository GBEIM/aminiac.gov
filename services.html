National Insurance

Healthcare, pensions, unemployment benefits, and disability support for all residents and workers

−

Who Needs to Contribute?

All employed individuals aged 18 to 67, self-employed workers, and employers must make regular contributions to the National Insurance system. Contributions are automatically deducted from salaries for employed workers, while self-employed individuals must file quarterly payments.

Apply for National Insurance

Register for a National Insurance number and start your coverage. This is required for all new residents and workers entering the Aminoac workforce.

Start Application

Check Your Contributions

View your contribution history, verify payments, and ensure your account is up to date. Access your annual contribution statement online.

Check Contributions

Claim Benefits

File claims for healthcare reimbursements, unemployment benefits, pension payments, or disability support through our secure online portal.

File a Claim

Tax Payment Services

Progressive tax system funding public services, infrastructure, and social programs

−

Important Tax Deadlines

Individual income tax returns must be filed by April 30th each year. Corporate tax returns are due by June 30th. Quarterly estimated tax payments are required for self-employed individuals and businesses. Late filing may result in penalties and interest charges.

File Income Tax

Submit your annual personal income tax return online. The system pre-fills information from employer reports and allows you to claim deductions and credits.

File Tax Return

Corporate Tax Filing

Business owners can file corporate tax returns, report annual revenue, claim business expenses, and manage quarterly estimated payments.

Business Tax Portal

Pay Outstanding Taxes

Make secure online payments for any outstanding tax obligations. Set up payment plans if you cannot pay the full amount immediately.

Make Payment

Cost of Living Support

Energy subsidies, housing assistance, food programs, and other support for managing living expenses

−

Eligibility Criteria

Most cost of living support programs are available to households earning below 150% of the national median income. Families with children, senior citizens, and individuals with disabilities may qualify for additional assistance. Eligibility is determined through an online assessment that considers household size, income, assets, and special circumstances.

Energy Subsidy Program

Receive monthly credits on your electricity and heating bills. The subsidy amount is based on household size and income, with additional support during winter months.

Apply for Subsidy

Housing Assistance

Access rental assistance, mortgage payment relief, and social housing programs. Help is available for those struggling with housing costs or facing homelessness.

Get Housing Help

Food Support Programs

Apply for food vouchers that can be used at participating supermarkets. Low-income families and seniors can receive monthly allocations to purchase groceries.

Request Food Vouchers

Childcare Subsidies

Working parents can receive up to 70% subsidy on licensed childcare and preschool costs.

Social Benefits

Income support, disability allowances, family benefits, and emergency assistance programs

−

Application Process

Complete the online benefits assessment to determine which programs you qualify for. The system will guide you through providing necessary documentation such as identification, income statements, and medical records where applicable. Applications are typically processed within 14 business days, and you will receive notification of the decision via email and postal mail.

Income Support

Apply for income assistance if you are unable to work due to illness, caregiving responsibilities, or temporary hardship. Monthly payments help cover basic living expenses.

Apply Now

Special Topics

Life event services covering birth, marriage, education, housing, driving, elderly care, and end-of-life matters

−

Life Events Made Simple

Our Special Topics section provides comprehensive guidance through major life events and specialized services. Each service includes step-by-step instructions, required documentation, processing timelines, and direct access to online applications. Services are organized to help you navigate important milestones efficiently.

Birth Registration

Register the birth of your child and obtain an official birth certificate. Parents must register newborns within 42 days of birth. Online registration is available, or visit any civil registry office with hospital birth records and parental identification.

Register a Birth

Parenting Education

Access free parenting courses, workshops, and counseling services for new and expectant parents. Programs cover child development, positive discipline, nutrition, safety, and family wellness. Both in-person and online sessions available.

View Parenting Programs

Special Education Services

Support and resources for children with learning disabilities, developmental delays, or special educational needs. Apply for individualized education plans, specialized teaching support, therapy services, and adaptive learning tools.

Access Special Education

Marriage Registration

Register your marriage and obtain an official marriage certificate. Book appointments for civil ceremonies, submit required documents, and access pre-marriage counseling services. Same-day registration available at select registry offices.

Register Marriage

Reproductive Health Services

Comprehensive reproductive health support including family planning counseling, prenatal care programs, fertility treatment subsidies, and maternal health services. Free consultations and screening programs available nationwide.

Health Services

Property Services

Property registration, title searches, land transactions, and property tax services. File building permit applications, register property ownership transfers, and access property valuation services online.

Property Services Portal

Driving Services

Apply for driver's licenses, renew existing licenses, schedule driving tests, and register vehicles. Access online services for license renewal, address changes, traffic fine payments, and driving record requests.

Driving & Vehicles

Elderly Services & Welfare

Comprehensive support services for senior citizens including home care assistance, meal delivery programs, social activities, health monitoring, and companionship services. Eligibility assessment available online.

Elderly Support Services

Elderly Housing

Apply for subsidized senior housing, assisted living facilities, and nursing homes. Information on age-friendly housing modifications, rent subsidies for elderly tenants, and community care facilities.

Senior Housing Options

Register a death and obtain death certificates. Access bereavement support services, funeral assistance programs, and guidance on estate settlement. Death must be registered within 5 days with medical certificate.

Register a Death
