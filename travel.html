Visa Services

Application and information for foreign nationals visiting Aminoac

Tourist Visa

Apply for a short-term tourist visa valid for up to 90 days. Required for citizens of countries not on the visa-free list. Processing time is typically 10-15 business days.

Apply for Tourist Visa

Business Visa

For individuals traveling to Aminoac for business meetings, conferences, or negotiations. Valid for single or multiple entries over a 6-month period.

Apply for Business Visa

Transit Visa

Required for travelers passing through Aminoac international airports with layovers exceeding 24 hours or those leaving the airport transit area.

Apply for Transit Visa

Check Visa Application Status

Track the progress of your visa application using your reference number. Receive real-time updates on document review and approval status.

Track Application

Visa Extension

Request an extension of your current visa if you need to stay longer than originally planned. Applications must be submitted at least 7 days before visa expiration.

Request Extension

Passport Services

Apply for new passports, renewals, and emergency travel documents for Aminoac citizens

New Passport Application

First-time passport applicants can submit applications online or at designated passport offices. Standard processing takes 4-6 weeks, with expedited service available for urgent travel.

Apply for New Passport

Passport Renewal

Renew your Aminoac passport up to 12 months before expiration. Online renewal is available for eligible citizens with no changes to personal information.

Renew Passport

Emergency Travel Document

Citizens abroad who have lost or had their passport stolen can obtain an emergency travel document at the nearest Aminoac embassy or consulate for immediate return home.

Request Emergency Document

Child Passport Services

Special passport services for minors under 16 years of age. Both parents or legal guardians must provide consent for passport issuance.

Apply for Child Passport

Travel Advisories & Safety Information

Current travel warnings and safety recommendations for Aminoac citizens abroad

Level 1 - Normal

Western Europe

Exercise normal precautions when traveling to Western European countries. No specific threats identified.

Level 2 - Caution

Eastern Europe

Exercise increased caution due to regional political tensions. Avoid demonstrations and large public gatherings.

Level 3 - Reconsider

Middle East (Selected Areas)

Reconsider travel due to ongoing conflicts and terrorist activities. Essential travel only with comprehensive security planning.

Level 4 - Do Not Travel

Active Conflict Zones

Do not travel to active war zones and areas under emergency declarations. Immediate evacuation recommended for current residents.
